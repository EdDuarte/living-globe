The files in this folder correspond to the original, unparsed data used in the
final visualization. The contents of these files contain data for multiple
countries, from the year 1960 to 2014, of the following indicators:

SP.POP.0014.TO.ZS - Population ages 0-14 (% of total)
SP.POP.1564.TO.ZS - Population ages 15-64 (% of total)
SP.POP.65UP.TO.ZS - Population ages 65 and above (% of total)
SP.POP.GROW - Population growth (annual %)
SP.DYN.CBRT.IN - Birth rate, crude (per 1,000 people)
SP.DYN.CDRT.IN - Death rate, crude (per 1,000 people)
SP.DYN.LE00.IN - Life expectancy at birth, total (years)


SM.POP.NETM - Net migration
